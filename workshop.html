Programming Tools for
Statistical Modelling

High - End Workshop

Workshop is aimed to provide hands-on experience to the students in practical knowledge of programming languages like Python, Matlab and R language for statistical modelling and skill development on themes required for research work. The program is meant to support motivated PG students and Ph.D. scholars, who are having a strong willingness to get excellence in their scientific and engineering research pursuits in the field of Data Collection, Statistical Modelling, Regression Modelling. The objective of the workshop is to improve research productivity of promising PG students and PhD scholars by training them on programming tools for statistical modelling.

Contents to be covered

  • Data Collection and Processing with python
  • Simulation Modelling in Python
  • Statistical Analysis Using MATLAB
  • Applied Statistical modelling for Data Analysis in R
  • Statistics With Ruby: Time Series and General Linear Models
  • Stochastic Numerics and Statistical Learning: Theory and Applications
  • Deep Neural Network (DNN)

Who Can Attend


Workshop is open to PG students and PhD scholars from Institutes, Colleges, and Universities in India. Applicants pursuing PG/PhD programs in any discipline of Engineering are eligible. Candidates who have completed PhD will not be considered
