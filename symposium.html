Amity School of Engineering and Technology (ASET), one of the pioneer institutions in the field of Engineering & Technology is organizing 3rd International Conference on Information Technology (InCITe-2023) on 2nd and 3rd March 2023. The Ph.D. Symposium is being organized during this International Conference on Information Technology (InCITe2023). Ph.D. awarded scholars during last one year of Institutes of National importance like IITs, IIITs, NITs and any other Research Institute or University are invited to send the abstract of their finished, summarized thesis for the Ph.D. Symposium. The Ph.D. Symposium aims to give scholars from all over the world a chance to showcase their research in Computer Science and Information Technology.
